PixelVault - Scope of Work
Category: Web Development

1. IN-SCOPE REQUIREMENTS:
- A web page with a header navigation bar, a main hero banner, a three card game catalog, a statistics dashboard area, a task modal window to play games, and a footer.
- A dark layout using four brand colors (hex codes #0F0F10, #2A2B30, #FFC107, and #9C27B0).
- Google Fonts (Orbitron for headers and Inter for card details and text).
- Three playable games:
  * Rock Paper Scissors: Selection buttons for user, random computer choice, win or loss checks, and score counts.
  * Flappy Bird: Canvas element drawing loop, spacebar or click to jump, pipes spawn as obstacles, speed increases, and pipe/floor collision crashes.
  * Word Scramble: Letter scrambling logic, user text typing input, check button, hint reveal, and score updates.
- Statistics Dashboard: Displays total games played, wins count, high score, and correct words count.
- Local Storage: Saves all statistics under the key named "pixelvault_stats" so data remains after a page reload.
- Reset Button: A button to clear all statistics from local storage and update the counters on the page immediately.
- Global Mute Toggle: Navbar button to turn off and on all background tracks and click or score sound effects.

2. OUT-OF-SCOPE ITEMS (EXCLUDED):
- Multiplayer features using databases or web sockets.
- Server-side backends (Node.js, Express, databases, user account logins).
- Online payments or ads.
- 3D visual graphics or frameworks (Three.js, Phaser).
